Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a hill station in Tamil Nadu known for its picturesque tea estates, gardens, and pleasant weather.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry, now known as Puducherry, is a charming coastal town with a unique blend of French and Indian culture, famous for its beaches and colonial architecture.

Shimla

Shimla is the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, renowned for its snow-capped peaks, Victorian architecture, and as a popular hill station getaway.

Kerala

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is famous for its backwaters, beaches, tea estates, and vibrant culture.

Goa

Goa, known for its pristine beaches and vibrant nightlife, offers a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement amidst Portuguese colonial architecture and rich cultural heritage.

Manali

Manali: A scenic Himalayan retreat famous for adventure sports and stunning mountain vistas.
